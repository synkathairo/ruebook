Why the rue?

For long the rue plant has been considered therapeutic in its many uses: in those medicinal, it has been a healing remedy; in those uses culinary, it yields a taste bitter and unpleasant; those who have written of it, have waxed prose and prosody. It is a plant with a story as complex as that of the human heart, and thus, inspired by such, we have chosen the name Ruebook for this journal application.

How do I use this app?

Use this journal to share your thoughts! Express yourself to receive helpful tips on practicing healthy mental health habits.
